Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

Simplay ("the App") is an IPTV application available on Apple TV and iOS. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

Information We Collect

The App collects the following types of information:

  • Analytics & Crash Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics and crash reports to help us improve app stability and performance. This may include device model, operating system version, app version, and general usage patterns.
  • Viewing History & Preferences: The App stores your viewing history and preferences (such as favorite channels and playback settings) locally on your device to enhance your experience. This data is not transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics (Google): We use Firebase to collect anonymous analytics and crash reports. Firebase may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Anonymous analytics data is shared only with Firebase (Google) for the purposes described above.

Data Retention

Analytics and crash data are retained in Firebase for up to 14 months. Viewing history and preferences are stored locally on your device and can be cleared at any time by deleting the App.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly remove it.

Your Choices

You can limit data collection by:

  • Disabling analytics sharing in your device's privacy settings.
  • Deleting the App to remove all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at legal@blv.sh.
