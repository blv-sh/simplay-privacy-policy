1. Introduction

Simplay ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") is an IPTV application available on Apple TV and iOS. This Privacy Policy explains how and why we collect, store, use, disclose, and otherwise process personal data when you use the Simplay application and any related services (collectively, our "Services").

By using our Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the practices described herein, please do not use our Services.

If you have concerns about providing data to us or how it is used as described in this Privacy Policy, please see Section 7 (Control Over Your Information) and Section 8 (Your Privacy Rights), or contact us at legal@blv.sh.

2. What Data We Collect and How We Collect It

Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Services, we may automatically collect and process the following types of data:

  • Analytics & Crash Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics and crash reports to improve app stability and performance. This may include your device model, operating system and version, app version, session duration, general usage patterns, crash logs, and diagnostic information such as loading times, latency, framerate, and other technical diagnostics.
  • Device Data: We may collect device-specific information such as your device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers (including Identifier for Vendor ("IDFV") on iOS and Apple TV), time zone, language settings, and screen resolution.
  • IP Address & Approximate Location: We may collect your Internet Protocol ("IP") address, which may be used to determine your approximate geographic location (country or region level). This is used for analytics purposes and to comply with content licensing requirements.
  • Viewing Activity: We collect information about your viewing history, playback preferences (such as audio and subtitle settings, video quality preferences, and equalizer settings), favorite channels, and content interaction patterns. This data is primarily stored locally on your device and is used to personalize and improve your experience.

Information Collected from Third Parties

We may receive certain data from third parties in connection with your use of our Services:

  • App Marketplaces: When you download our application or make purchases through the Apple App Store, we receive information about your device, account (such as a transaction receipt), and payment confirmations. We do not receive access to your credit card, bank account, or other financial account data when you make purchases through the App Store. The data we receive from Apple is subject to their privacy policies and your settings.
  • Content Providers: If you connect third-party IPTV or streaming services within the App, we may receive limited information necessary to authenticate and deliver the content you have requested.

Information We Do Not Collect

Simplay does not collect the following:

  • Your name, email address, or other contact information (unless you voluntarily provide it to us, for example when contacting support)
  • Credit card or banking information
  • Precise geolocation data (GPS)
  • Contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data
  • Data from other apps on your device

3. Purposes for Processing Your Data

We may use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our Services, including delivering IPTV content, managing your viewing preferences, and ensuring playback functionality across Apple TV and iOS;
  • To personalize your experience, including remembering your favorite channels, playback settings, audio equalizer preferences, and viewing history;
  • To improve and optimize the performance, stability, and usability of our Services, including identifying and fixing bugs and crashes;
  • To process and verify in-app purchases and subscriptions, and to ensure that features and content you have paid for are properly activated;
  • To understand how users interact with our Services through aggregated and anonymous analytics, allowing us to make informed decisions about feature development;
  • To communicate with you regarding service-related information, including technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages when you contact us;
  • To prevent potentially prohibited or illegal activities, including unauthorized access, fraud, and misuse of our Services;
  • To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or governmental requests;
  • To establish, exercise, or defend our legal rights.

4. Subscriptions and Purchases

Simplay offers the following types of in-app purchases:

  • Auto-Renewable Subscriptions: We offer two subscription plans that provide access to premium features and content. Subscriptions are billed on a recurring basis (depending on the plan selected) and automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period.
  • Non-Consumable Purchase: We offer a one-time, non-consumable purchase that permanently unlocks specific features or content. This purchase is tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device signed in with the same Apple ID.

All purchases and subscriptions are processed exclusively through the Apple App Store. We do not process payments directly and do not have access to your payment method details. We receive only a transaction receipt from Apple confirming the purchase, which we use to validate and activate the corresponding features or content in the App.

For information about managing your subscriptions, including how to cancel, please visit Apple's subscription management guide.

We may retain anonymized purchase data (such as which product was purchased and when) for analytics, accounting, and legal compliance purposes.

5. Disclosure of Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. We may disclose data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our Services, such as analytics providers and cloud infrastructure services. These providers are contractually obligated to use data only as necessary to perform services on our behalf and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
  • Legal Requirements: We may access, preserve, and disclose your data if we believe doing so is required or appropriate to: (i) comply with law enforcement or national security requests and legal processes, such as a court order or subpoena; (ii) protect your, our, or others' rights, property, or safety; (iii) enforce our policies or terms; or (iv) when we believe disclosure is necessary to prevent harm or in connection with an investigation of suspected illegal activity.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, bankruptcy, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via a prominent notice within our Services of any change in ownership or uses of your personal data.
  • With Your Consent: We may disclose data about you to third parties when we have your explicit consent to do so.

6. Third-Party Services

Our Services use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google): We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics, understand how users interact with our app, and identify trends. Firebase may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy and Firebase Privacy Information.
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google): We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostic data to help us identify and fix issues. This may include device state information, crash stack traces, and device identifiers. Data collected by Crashlytics is governed by Firebase Crashlytics Terms.

Our Services may contain links to third-party websites, content providers, or services. This Privacy Policy does not apply to, and we are not responsible for, the privacy practices of any third-party services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you access through or in connection with our App.

7. Control Over Your Information

You have several options to control how your data is handled:

  • Delete or Reinstall the App: If you delete Simplay from your device, we will no longer collect data via the App on that device. Locally stored data (such as viewing history, favorites, and preferences) will be removed. However, we may still retain anonymous analytics data that was collected prior to deletion.
  • Restore Purchases: Non-consumable purchases and active subscriptions are tied to your Apple ID. You can restore them at any time by reinstalling the App and using the restore purchases feature.
  • Limit Analytics Collection: You can limit analytics data collection by adjusting your device privacy settings. On iOS and Apple TV, go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements and disable sharing analytics data.
  • Limit Ad Tracking: Although Simplay does not currently display advertisements, you can limit ad tracking on your device via Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking on iOS, or the equivalent setting on Apple TV.
  • Reset Advertising Identifier: You can reset your device's advertising identifier at any time through your device's privacy settings.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on the privacy laws applicable in your jurisdiction, you may have some or all of the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: You may have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: You may have the right to request that we correct or complete any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Deletion: You may have the right to request that we delete your personal data, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You may have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data under certain circumstances.
  • Right to Object: You may have the right to object to certain uses of your personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You may have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where we rely on your consent to process personal data, you may withdraw your consent at any time. Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before the withdrawal.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: You have the right not to receive discriminatory treatment for exercising your privacy rights.

To submit a request to exercise your privacy rights, please contact us at legal@blv.sh. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request. We will respond to all legitimate requests within the timeframes required by applicable law.

For Users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and Switzerland

If you are located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, we process your personal data on the following legal bases: (i) your consent; (ii) performance of a contract (to deliver the Services you have purchased); (iii) our legitimate interests (to improve our Services and ensure security); and (iv) compliance with legal obligations. You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe your data has been processed unlawfully.

For Users in California (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA). We do not sell your personal information as defined by the CCPA/CPRA. You have the right to request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal data we have collected, to request deletion, and to not be discriminated against for exercising your rights.

If you are located in Türkiye, your personal data is processed in accordance with the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 ("KVKK"). You have the right to learn whether your personal data has been processed, to request information about such processing, to learn the purpose of processing and whether data is used in accordance with its purpose, to know third parties to whom personal data has been transferred, to request correction of incomplete or inaccurate data, to request deletion or destruction of data, and to object to any outcome that arises from the analysis of data exclusively through automated systems. To exercise these rights, please contact us at legal@blv.sh.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under this age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal data to us, please contact us at legal@blv.sh so that we can take steps to remove such information and terminate the child's access to our Services.

If you are above the age of 13 but under the age of 18 (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction), you must obtain the consent of your parent or legal guardian before using our Services.

10. Data Security

We maintain physical, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption of data in transit, secure cloud infrastructure, access controls, and regular security assessments.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. We encourage you to take steps to protect your devices and accounts.

11. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including to satisfy any legal, regulatory, tax, accounting, or reporting requirements. Specifically:

  • Analytics and Crash Data: Retained in Firebase for up to 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted or anonymized.
  • Viewing History and Preferences: Stored locally on your device and removed when you delete the App.
  • Purchase and Subscription Records: Anonymized transaction records may be retained for up to 5 years for accounting and legal compliance purposes.

Once personal data is no longer necessary for the purposes described above or required by law, we delete or anonymize it.
